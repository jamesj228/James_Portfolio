About Me

Interests

I have always been fascinated with technology because of its mysterious nature. I used to take my toys apart because I wanted to see what they were made up of and how was it possible to for these small components to work together and create and action so complex. This curiosity eventually led me to computers and I remembering being blown away by the how much a computer do. I had no idea that computers ran on programs because by the time I started using computers, there was already GUI from windows XP. But my curiosity strayed away when I started playing video games and all I wanted to do was build an empire in StarCraft or get some headshots in CounterStrike.

It only took me 9 years when i was a senior in High School to finally start having interest in how computers worked again. I was really into the hardware part of technology but I soon realized the software was the heart and soul that drove the computer. The blue screens of death, dial up, and even the lag in games were the result of software. I am very grateful to have made the choice to major in informatics because this is the major I really enjoy learning the most. Although I struggle in some subjects, there is a sense of reward when I finally do understand the problem that I wouldn't get from any other majors.
